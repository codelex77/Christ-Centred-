Our Story

A Ministry Born of Divine Purpose

Christ Centered Evangelistic Ministries was founded in 1998 in Selebe-Phikwe, Botswana — a ministry born out of a sovereign move of God. What began as a local flame grew into a regional fire, touching Botswana, Zimbabwe, Zambia, and South Africa with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

The Lord has been glorified through outstanding miracles, healing, deliverance, prophecy, and the raising up of ministers for the Gospel. Today, the ministry continues to rebuild and grow, with its home of worship in Gabane.

The Overseer

Apostle Kaisara G

Called of God and highly anointed to minister the Word with divine mystery revelation, Apostle Kaisara G serves as the Overseer of Christ Centered Evangelistic Ministries. A spiritual son of Senior Prophet Samuel Kakande of Uganda, he carries a mantle of signs, wonders, and the building of God's Kingdom.

1972

Born on 7 July in Tonota, Botswana.

1993

Received salvation in Selebe-Phikwe. A life surrendered to Christ.

1994

Ordained as pastor. Called and commissioned for the work of the Gospel.

1995

Enrolled at World Harvest Theology College, Germiston Primrose Hill, Johannesburg. Graduated with a Diploma in Theology.

1998

Founded and registered Christ Centered Evangelistic Ministries in Botswana — the beginning of a movement that would reach four nations.
